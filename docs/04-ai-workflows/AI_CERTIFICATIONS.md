## Microsoft

Official cert portal: **https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/**

Microsoft moved to a role-based credential system. Exams cost ~$165 USD. Certifications require renewal every year via a free online assessment (no re-exam fee).

**Status key:** ✅ Active | 🆕 New / launching | ⚠️ Recently updated | 🔄 Retiring

### AI and Data — Fundamentals

| Cert | Exam | Level | Cost | Status | Replaced By |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Azure AI Fundamentals | AI-900 | Beginner | ~$165 | 🔄 Retiring Jun 30 2026 | AI-901 |
| **AI Fundamentals (new)** | **AI-901** | Beginner | ~$165 | 🆕 Launching Jun 2026 | — |
| Azure Data Fundamentals | DP-900 | Beginner | ~$165 | ✅ Active | — |
| Power Platform Fundamentals | PL-900 | Beginner | ~$165 | ✅ Active | — |

> **AI-901** replaces AI-900. It adds Copilot, responsible AI, and Azure AI Foundry content. If you are starting a Microsoft AI track in 2026, target AI-901 directly.

### AI and Data — Associate / Expert

> **AI-103** (Azure AI Services Agent Associate) replaces AI-102 with heavier emphasis on agentic AI development, multi-agent systems, and Azure AI Foundry.

### New in 2026 — AB Series (Launched Feb 2026)

Microsoft launched a new "Applied Business" badge series aimed at AI for everyday business users — not engineers.

| Credential | Exam | Focus | Cost |

### Microsoft Applied Skills (shorter, task-based — free to earn, no exam fee)

| Credential | Focus | Cost |

## Google Cloud

Official cert portal: **https://cloud.google.com/learn/certification**

Exams cost ~$99–$200 USD. Valid for 2 years, renewal by recertification.

| Cert | Level | Cost | Status | Replaces |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cloud Digital Leader | Foundational | ~$200 | ✅ Active | — |
| Associate Cloud Engineer | Associate | ~$200 | ✅ Active | — |
| Professional Machine Learning Engineer | Professional | ~$200 | ✅ Active | — |
| Professional Data Engineer | Professional | ~$200 | ✅ Active | — |
| Professional Cloud Developer | Professional | ~$200 | ✅ Active | — |
| **Generative AI Leader** | Professional | ~$99 | 🆕 Launched May 2025 | — |

> **Generative AI Leader** (launched May 2025) is Google's dedicated GenAI credential. It covers LLM design, Vertex AI, responsible AI, and enterprise GenAI strategy. Lower price point (~$99) than traditional Professional exams makes it a good entry point for the Google AI track.

### Google Free Learning Paths (no cert, but structured)

| Path | Focus | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Generative AI Learning Path | Gemini, Vertex AI, prompt engineering | Free |
| Introduction to Generative AI | Foundational overview | Free |

## AWS

Official cert portal: **https://aws.amazon.com/certification/**

Exams cost $100–$300 USD. Valid for 3 years.

| Cert | Exam | Level | Cost | Status | Replaced By |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| AWS Certified AI Practitioner | AIF-C01 | Foundational | ~$100 | ✅ Active (2024) | — |
| **AWS Certified AI Practitioner+** | **AIP-C01** | Associate | ~$150 | 🆕 2026 (replaces ML Specialty) | — |
| AWS Certified ML Engineer – Associate | MLA-C01 | Associate | ~$150 | ✅ Active (2024) | — |
| AWS Certified Data Engineer – Associate | DEA-C01 | Associate | ~$150 | ✅ Active | — |
| AWS Certified Machine Learning – Specialty | MLS-C01 | Specialty | ~$300 | 🔄 Retired Mar 2026 | AIP-C01 |

> **AIF-C01** (AI Practitioner) is the starting point for the AWS AI track — foundational, $100. **MLS-C01** (ML Specialty) retired March 2026 and is replaced by **AIP-C01** (AI Practitioner+), which covers advanced GenAI, RAG, model evaluation, and responsible AI at associate/specialty depth.

| Cert | Level | Cost | Status |

| OCI Generative AI Professional | Professional | ~$245 | ✅ Active (2024) |

## NVIDIA

NVIDIA offers course completions and graded assessments. Not traditional vendor certifications but respected in ML/infra roles.

| Course / Credential | Focus | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Fundamentals of Deep Learning | Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs | ~$30–$90 |
| Building Transformer-Based NLP | Transformers, BERT, fine-tuning | ~$30–$90 |

## CompTIA

**https://www.comptia.org/certifications**

| Cert | Exam | Level | Cost | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CompTIA SecAI+** | CY0-001 | Intermediate | ~$239 | 🆕 Launched Feb 2026 | AI security, governance, risk |
| CompTIA AI Essentials | — | Beginner | Free | ✅ Active | Non-proctored awareness course |

> **SecAI+** (CY0-001, launched February 2026) is CompTIA's proctored AI credential. It covers AI threat modeling, secure AI deployment, governance, ethics, and compliance. Aimed at security practitioners working with AI systems. Valid 3 years, renewal by CE credits or retesting.
>
> **CompTIA AI Essentials** is a free, self-paced awareness course — not a proctored exam. Good for non-technical stakeholders.
>
> Note: "CompTIA AI+" was listed in older sources as a standalone cert — that exam code did not become a shipping proctored certification. SecAI+ is the actual proctored product.

## Choosing a Path

**Starting from zero, no cloud background:**
→ Google AI Essentials (free) → Azure AI-901 (replaces AI-900, Jun 2026) → then pick a cloud platform

**Already a developer, want AI credibility:**
→ DeepLearning.AI Generative AI with LLMs → AWS AI Practitioner (AIF-C01) or Azure AI-103

**Want to run models locally / open-source path:**
→ fast.ai Practical Deep Learning → Hugging Face Course → HuggingFace fine-tuning track

**Cloud infrastructure focus:**
→ Pick your platform (Azure/AWS/GCP), get the foundational cert, then the ML/AI specialty

**Vendor-neutral / security and governance focus:**
→ CompTIA SecAI+ → IBM AI Engineering (Coursera)

**Microsoft ecosystem:**
→ AI-901 (launching Jun 2026, replaces AI-900) → AI-103 (launching Jun 2026, replaces AI-102)

**Google AI focus:**
→ Cloud Digital Leader → Generative AI Leader (~$99) → Professional ML Engineer
