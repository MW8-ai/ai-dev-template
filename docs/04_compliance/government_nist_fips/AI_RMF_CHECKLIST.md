# AI_RMF_CHECKLIST.md

## Purpose

Apply NIST AI RMF-style thinking to AI-assisted development.

## Govern

- Who owns AI tool usage?
- What tools are approved?
- What data can be shared?
- Are outputs reviewed?
- Are risky actions gated?

## Map

- What tasks use AI?
- What data can agents access?
- What systems can agents modify?
- What failure modes exist?

## Measure

- Are generated changes tested?
- Are hallucinations caught?
- Are security findings tracked?
- Are model costs tracked?
- Are incidents reviewed?

## Manage

- Are permissions limited?
- Are logs retained?
- Are high-risk tasks human-approved?
- Are prompts/instructions updated after repeated failures?
- Are tool snapshots refreshed?
